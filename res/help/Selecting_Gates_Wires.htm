Selecting Gates/Wires

1. Place the mouse over the gate or wire that you wish to select and left click. The gate or wire will be stippled when it is selected. Note that when your cursor is over a wire and you are able to select it, a red x will appear. If you left click on a wire when a red x appears on it, it will be selected. Once a gate or wire is selected, you can left click anywhere on the canvas to de-select it. The figure below illustrates a selected gate.

2. To select multiple gates, hold down the left mouse button and drag it to form a blue highlighting box over the gates and wires you wish to select.

3. Release the left mouse button and the group of gates and wires that where inside the blue box area will be selected. To unselect them, simply left click anywhere on the canvas.

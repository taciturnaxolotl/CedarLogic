Manipulating Selections Of Gates/Wires

Deleting Gates/Wires

1. To delete gates and wires, select the ones which you wish to delete and press the delete key. Note that when a gate is deleted, all wires connected to it are also deleted.

 

Moving Gates/Wires

1. To move a gate/wire, simply select it and drag it anywhere on the screen you would like. Note that CEDAR logic has rubberbanding, which means that the wires stay connected to a gate even when it is moved.

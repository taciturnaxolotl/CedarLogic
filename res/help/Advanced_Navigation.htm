Advanced Navigation

1. CEDAR logic offers several advanced zooming features which aide in navigation. In this section of the tutorial, we will cover the basics of navigation in CEDAR logic.

2. The +/- buttons on the CEDAR logic toolbar and on the keyboard enable you to do a basic zoom in/out of a circuit.

3. Press the + key 5 times to zoom into the circuit as shown.

4. The mini-map can be used to navigate around a large circuit or one that you are zoomed in on.

5. To use the mini-map, simply click on it and drag the red box in the mini-map to the appropriate place you wish to be on the circuit.

6. Use the mini-map to navigate the two full-adder circuits until you have become comfortable using the mini-map feature of the program.

7. Once you have become comfortable with the mini-map, press the space bar to re-center the full-adder circuit on your display. The space bar can be used to re-center a circuit at any time during a simulation.

8. Save the changes you have made to this circuit as you will need it for the next tutorial.

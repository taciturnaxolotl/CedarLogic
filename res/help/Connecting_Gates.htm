Connecting Gates

1. Place the mouse cursor over the output or input of one of the gates you are connecting until a red box (hotspot) appears over the input or output.

2. Left click on the hotspot. A green wire will appear when the hotspot is clicked on. While continuing to hold down the left mouse button, drag the wire towards the input/output of the appropriate gate.

3. Continue to drag the wire until the hotspot of the output/input of the other gate appears.

4. Release the left mouse button. The gates will now be connected by a wire.
